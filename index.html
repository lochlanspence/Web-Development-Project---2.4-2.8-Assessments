Your accommodation

This architecturaly designed luxury vacation rental near Wanaka was completed in 2022. A certified Passive House the property looks out over beautiful Lake Wanaka and surrounding snow-capped mountains.

Surrounded by tussocks at the end of a winding gravel road, this single level dwelling opens onto a wrap around deck, connecting with a schist flagstone patio featuring al-fresco sitting and dining areas. The hot tub nestles into the patio with jaw drropping views across the lake while you can ease weary legs from a day of adventuring in the hills.
